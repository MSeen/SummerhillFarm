Accommodation


You have a choice of accommodation styles at Summerhill Farm: the cosy and casual Blue Cottage or the more traditional Edwardian Suite.


The Blue Cottage

Secluded in its own private corner of the rambling country garden, The Blue Cottage (circa 1940s) is relaxed and comfy  - perfect for a weekend getaway.

The Blue Cottage consists of a queen bedroom with en-suite, sitting/dining room and small kitchen. It includes a TV and CD player, and a cosy wood-fired heater for chilly evenings. Breakfast ingredients supplied; bring your own fresh ingredients for additional meals.

A porta-cot and a fold-up single mattress (within the ottoman) are available.  Additional stretchers can be supplied - please ask.

A kennel is available for when your dog is on holiday with you.  Well-behaved dogs are permitted in the Blue Cottage.

The Edwardian Suite

Situated within the old homestead, The Edwardian Suite is traditionally furnished and gives access to the verandah - ideal for morning coffee or an evening glass of local wine.  

The Edwardian Suite consists of a Queen bedroom with en-suite and a spacious sitting room with TV and CD player.
 
Ingredients are provided for a generous Continental-style breakfast (cereal, toast and fruit).  No cooking facilities but a  fridge, microwave, kettle and toaster are available. 

For an extra charge the Rose Room (another queen bed and a fold-out double sofa) can be included with the Edwardian Suite, allowing accommodation for two couples or extended family.

Additional cots and stretchers can be supplied - please ask.

Pets are not allowed inside the Edwardian Suite.
