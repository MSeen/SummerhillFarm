About Us

Summerhill Farm is situated on 10 peaceful acres at Main Ridge, 75 minutes from Melbourne CBD, in the heart of the picturesque Mornington Peninsula.


Owned by Beverley Baker, Summerhill Farm is a working alpaca stud.  We breed and show alpacas, shear them and sell the fleeces and yarns.


While we do not provide farming activities, guests are welcome to wander about and see our variety of animals - alpacas, horses, chickens, geese, peacocks and "Boss Cocky & The Missus" - our very own visiting cockatoos. We also provide a refuge for rescue animals. Feel free to say hello to the Shetland pony Copperpot, our latest addition.


Summerhill Farm is a beautiful place to visit at any time of the year.  Each season has its own appeal, and we love sharing our special part of the world.
