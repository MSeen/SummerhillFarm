Near Us

Only a 75 minute drive from Melbourne CBD, we are close to beaches, wineries & restaurants, golf courses, fromageries, craft markets, high-ropes courses, national parks, tea rooms, galleries and beautiful display gardens.

Whether immersing yourself in the farm or enjoying the many and varied local attractions, relax and unwind in our lovely area.

Here are some of our favourite local places.  Please ask if you have any questions.  Local transport options if you don't want to drive yourself include the Ridge Rider Shuttle Bus and MP Designated Drivers.
