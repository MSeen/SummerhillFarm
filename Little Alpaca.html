Little Alpaca


'Little Alpaca' started as an on-farm outlet at Summerhill Farm and has since grown to two boutiques in two beautiful locations - Flinders on the Mornington Peninsula and Olinda in the Dandenong Ranges.  Both locations specialise in luxury clothing, accessories and homewares of natural fibres, and of course, feature Summalon Alpaca fleeces and yarns.

Guests at Summerhill Farm B&B receive a voucher entitling them to a 10% discount on purchases from Little Alpaca.
