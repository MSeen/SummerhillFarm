Wedding & Photo Shoots

Over the years Summerhill Farm has been the location for wedding photos,  an Australian ABC TV drama and promotional videos for fashion apparel.  If you are looking for a rural backdrop for your special requirements, we offer both the garden and paddocks (with or without curious animals) for $200/hour.  Bookings essential.  Enquiries and inspection welcome.
